DTU Sports Complex

Welcome to the DTU Sports Complex

The DTU Sports Complex is a state-of-the-art facility designed to cater to the sporting needs of students and staff. With world-class infrastructure and facilities, it promotes physical fitness and sportsmanship.

Facilities

The sports complex includes facilities for various sports such as:

  • Cricket
  • Football
  • Basketball
  • Badminton
  • Table Tennis
  • Gymnasium
