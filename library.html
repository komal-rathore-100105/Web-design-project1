About the Library

Delhi Technological University (DTU), one of India's premier engineering institutions, has a well-established library known as the Central Library, which plays a crucial role in supporting the academic and research activities of its students, faculty, and staff.

The DTU Library is dedicated to providing quality resources, research support, and a conducive learning environment.

Library Collection

  • Books
  • E-Journals
  • Databases
  • Theses & Dissertations

The library houses an impressive collection of academic resources, including over 180,000 books, journals, and periodicals in both print and digital formats. It covers diverse fields such as engineering, science, humanities, and management to support DTU's various disciplines.

Library Services

  • Reference & Consultation:

    The library staff assists with resource discovery, literature reviews, and research assistance.

  • Inter-Library Loan:

    For materials not available in the library, inter-library loans with other institutions may be arranged.

  • E-Resources and Digital Library:

    The DTU library subscribes to a wide range of e-journals, databases, and e-books, which are accessible to students and faculty.

    Digital resources include access to platforms like IEEE Xplore, JSTOR, ScienceDirect, and SpringerLink, offering a global knowledge base.

  • Study Spaces:

    The library has dedicated spaces for silent study, group discussions, and project work.

Advanced Facilities

Equipped with Wi-Fi, desktop computers, and multimedia resources.

A Digital Library Section allows students to access online journals, databases, and institutional repositories.

Library Hours

The library remains open seven days a week, with extended hours during exam seasons to support student needs.
