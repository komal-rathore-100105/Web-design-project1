Welcome to SBI DTU Branch

We offer various financial services to support the students and faculty at DTU.

The DTU Bank is more than just a banking facility; it’s a cornerstone of the campus community, designed to cater to the diverse financial needs of students, faculty, and staff. Conveniently located within the university, it offers a comprehensive range of services, including savings and current accounts, loans, and investment options.

About Us

State Bank of India, DTU branch, is dedicated to providing reliable banking services to the DTU community.

With user-friendly ATMs and robust online banking capabilities, accessing funds and managing finances has never been easier. The bank is committed to enhancing financial literacy among students, hosting engaging workshops and seminars that empower them with essential money management skills.

Our Services

  • Account opening
  • Educational loans
  • Online banking
  • 24/7 ATM service

In addition to its banking services, the DTU Bank fosters a sense of community by participating in campus events and initiatives, making it a trusted partner in students’ educational journeys. Its modern approach to banking, combined with personalized service, ensures that the DTU Bank is not just a place to conduct transactions but a valuable resource for financial growth and stability on campus.
